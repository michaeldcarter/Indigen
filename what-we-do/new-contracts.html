On October 31, 2019 Lane was awarded a design-build contract to widen the highway and install a dual express toll lane system on one of Washington's most traveled and congested corridors.

State Road 408 Widening
On July 17, 2017 Lane was awarded a $76 million contract for the State Road 408 (SR 408) Widening project from SR 417 to Alafaya Trail in Orange County, FL. More than 77,000 vehicles travel this stretch of SR 408 each day.

 

USA - New York

Unionport Bridge
On July 12, 2017, Lane, in a joint-venture partnership with Schiavone Construction Co., LLC, was awarded a $233 million contract for the Bruckner Expressway over Westchester Creek (Unionport Bridge) in Bronx County, New York.

 

USA - Washington, D.C.

Northeast Boundary Tunnel (NEBT)
On July 7, 2017, Lane and its parent company Salini Impregilo were awarded the $580 million Northeast Boundary Tunnel Project in Washington, D.C. The design-build contract includes the construction of the 27,000-foot long Northeast Boundary Tunnel, seven deep shafts, and diversion facilities.

 

USA - Indiana

The Three Rivers Protection & Overflow Reduction Tunnel (3RPORT)
On April 6, 2017, Lane was awarded a $188 million contract for the Three Rivers Protection & Overflow Reduction Tunnel (3RPORT) project. The project is a combined sewer overflow (CSO) tunnel located in Fort Wayne. It includes a deep rock tunnel, drop shafts, and consolidation sewers to collect and convey combined sewer overflow from eight locations along the St. Mary and Maumee Rivers. Once completed, the CSO tunnel system will reduce 90% of combined sewage overflows into the rivers, which occur during large rain storms.
 

 

USA - Virginia

395 Express Lanes
On March 1, 2017, Lane was awarded a contract for the 395 Express Lanes Extension. The project consists of an eight-mile extension of the 95 Express Lanes from Edsall Road in Fairfax County to Eads Street in Arlington County. The new construction will extend the 95 Express Lanes to the Washington D.C. line and is expected to significantly reduce congestion along the I-395 corridor.

On January 30, 2017, Lane was awarded a contract in relation to the Al Maktoum International Airport Expansion Project in Dubai, UAE. Lane will be working alongside the National Contracting Transportation Corp. of Sharjah, UAE, and will be responsible for 60% of the $125 million project for a value of $75 million.
